Value-Add Industrial

Breathing new life into outdated warehouses and flex space through targeted upgrades and modern re-leasing strategies.

Our Approach

Industrial real estate is at the core of supply chain growth — yet many properties lag in functionality and leasing. We target assets with strong locations but obsolete features, and reposition them with upgrades and new tenancy to capture today’s demand for logistics and flex space.

Where We Focus

Distribution and flex assets in high-demand corridors that suffer from deferred maintenance, outdated clear heights, inefficient loading, or tenant rollover risk. These buildings are often overlooked but can be transformed into competitive space with the right execution.

Execution Levers

  • Re-leasing to creditworthy tenants aligned with logistics, e-commerce, and light manufacturing
  • Capital improvements such as dock upgrades, roof replacements, and lighting retrofits
  • Reconfiguring sites for modern truck circulation and parking requirements
  • Subdivision or expansion to match tenant demand profiles
  • Disciplined lease structuring and capital deployment to stabilize long-term cash flow

Proven Playbook

Value-add industrial has reshaped markets across the U.S., with billions of dollars deployed to reposition outdated warehouses into competitive assets. Through industry peers and partners, we’ve seen this playbook executed successfully — from dock upgrades and tenant incentives to complete site overhauls. We apply those lessons with the same clarity and discipline that guide our broader investment platform.
