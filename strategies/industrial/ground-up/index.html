Land-Based Strategies

Ground-Up Industrial

Developing next-generation industrial facilities where tenant demand exceeds supply — from infill logistics to tilt-wall distribution space.

Our Approach

We pursue development opportunities in growth corridors and urban infill locations where tenant demand is underserved. Our goal is to fill market voids with projects sized and configured to meet today’s requirements — whether tilt-wall distribution space or flexible, smaller-format metal buildings.

Where We Focus

Sites with strong highway and infrastructure access, proximity to labor pools, and zoning that supports industrial use. We target infill parcels where supply is constrained and growing submarkets where quality product is scarce.

Execution Levers

  • Developing tilt-wall buildings in markets with shallow-bay oversupply
  • Delivering smaller-format, metal buildings in emerging corridors
  • Designing flexible layouts to accommodate both logistics and light manufacturing
  • Creating stabilized yards or crane-ready sites to serve specialized users

Proven Playbook

Industrial development has reshaped growth corridors across the U.S., driven by logistics, e-commerce, and reshoring trends. Through peers and partners, we’ve seen how ground-up execution — from site selection to tilt-wall delivery — consistently creates outsized value. We apply those lessons with discipline, targeting only the locations and formats where demand is deep and sustainable.
