Land-Based Strategies

Value-Add Retail

Transforming neglected retail centers into vibrant, income-producing assets through disciplined repositioning and re-tenanting.

Our Approach

We target centers where ownership has stopped investing, resulting in tired façades, high vacancy, and rents far below market comps. We welcome lease-up risk when the fundamentals are strong, and we deploy capital decisively to reposition these properties into attractive destinations for tenants and investors alike.

Where We Focus

Neighborhood and community centers in growth corridors with strong demographics, where execution has lagged. These assets often suffer from deferred maintenance, weak leasing, or outdated site plans — but have the bones to perform once repositioned.

Execution Levers

  • Re-tenanting with national credit and demand-driven tenants
  • Façade refreshes, parking lot restriping, LED lighting, and landscaping
  • Generous tenant improvements and leasing commissions to accelerate absorption
  • Outparcel development or site reconfiguration to unlock hidden value
  • Disciplined lease structuring to stabilize cash flows

Proven Playbook

Value-add retail is a well-established strategy that has reshaped millions of square feet of shopping centers across the country. By working alongside industry peers, we’ve seen firsthand how decisive capital deployment — from re-tenanting and façade refreshes to TI packages and parking lot upgrades — can transform underperforming centers into thriving community assets. We bring that playbook forward with the same clarity, discipline, and focus on long-term value that defines all of our investments.
