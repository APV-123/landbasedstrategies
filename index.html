About

Land-Based Strategies began in 2020 as a hands-on investment platform focused on land deals across Texas. What started as a simple way to source and market listings evolved into a broader mission: to build a scalable, systems-driven investment practice rooted in execution.

Since launch, I’ve led dozens of transactions—many under tight timelines, all with clear alignment on value. Along the way, I’ve developed Deal Studio, a proprietary software platform, and built lasting partnerships with operators, brokers, and capital sources.

Today, my focus is on institutional-quality acquisitions, strategic joint ventures, and leveraging data and technology to drive repeatable outcomes.

Investment Track Record

I’ve executed on millions of square feet across the Texas Triangle, with land transactions in counties including Bastrop, Brazoria, Harris, Hays, Hunt, Matagorda, Smith, Waller, and others—totaling over 100 acres acquired and sold to date.

What began as self-funded, unlevered land deals has matured into a broader platform—leveraging private credit, structuring joint ventures, and expanding through institutional relationships. I’ve also completed residential flips and seller-financed exits where the strategy called for it.

My edge lies in solving complexity: resolving heirship, clearing title, and engineering creative capital stacks to unlock value. Today, I’m working with Pearl River Companies to pursue ground-up and value-add opportunities in industrial, retail, and mixed-use product types across Texas.

Deal Studio

Deal Studio is a proprietary software platform I built to scale and streamline our investment operations. It serves as the backbone of our workflow—integrating data, process, and collaboration into a single, purpose-built system.

  • Scrapes and filters land listings across key markets
  • Enriches each opportunity with GIS, parcel, and market context
  • Tracks deals from sourcing through disposition
  • Enables team collaboration and investor reporting

Originally developed as an internal tool, Deal Studio is evolving into the operating system for a new generation of land investors—designed to support disciplined growth at scale.

Operating Principles

  • Operate with discipline. Process, rigor, and repeatability create lasting advantage.
  • Prioritize long-term alignment. Real trust takes time—and outperforms over time.
  • Rely on fundamentals. Sound judgment and clear math beat trends and tactics.
  • Invest in people. Great partnerships drive durable outcomes.
  • Be a good steward. Capital—whether time, money, or trust—should be treated with care.
