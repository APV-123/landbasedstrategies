Deal Studio

The CRM, analysis, and execution system for real estate — from sourcing to close — built for multifamily, mixed-use, industrial, retail, hospitality, and single-family communities across the Texas Triangle and the Hill Country.

Why Deal Studio

Real estate deals are messy: fragmented leads, manual enrichment, scattered diligence, and ad-hoc underwriting. Deal Studio centralizes the process — so teams can evaluate opportunities consistently and execute with discipline across ground-up development and value-add investments. See outcomes in the Track Record.

Core Features

Deal Studio isn’t a data feed — it’s an operating system. A single place to capture, qualify, underwrite, and advance deals with an auditable memory — covering Houston, Dallas–Fort Worth, Austin, San Antonio, and Hill Country counties.

Sourcing Hub

Ingest on- and off-market leads (brokerage, inbound, lists, driving, referrals) and normalize them into a consistent pipeline — searchable by geography, product type, and strategy.

Enrichment Engine

Auto-layer parcel, zoning, flood, comps, rent & sales benchmarks, demographics, and map context to get to a first read fast — regardless of asset class.

Diligence Workflow

Checklists, title/access reviews, entitlement items, vendor tasks, and document vault — structured to reduce closing risk and keep momentum.

Underwriting Memory

Inputs, assumptions, decision logs, and structures preserved — so you know what changed, why you advanced, and how a thesis evolved.

Collaboration & Reporting

Notes, assignments, updates, and scheduled reporting keep partners, lenders, and capital aligned — without spreadsheets and email chains.

No. It supports multifamily, mixed-use, industrial, retail, hospitality, and single-family communities.

Does it include data feeds?

It centralizes intake and enrichment; it’s an operating system, not a single data vendor.

Where is it used?

Across the Texas Triangle and the Hill Country, with workflows designed for ground-up and value-add.
